quest is a tiny cli for everyday adventures.

it keeps your tasks close,
finds good shows,
and stays out of your way.

no account.
no cloud oath.
just you and the terminal.

---

## the vibe

this project follows a simple spell:

- keep it local
- keep it clear
- keep it fun

your data lives on your machine in plain json.
commands do one job, then vanish back into the prompt.

---

## why quest exists

most tools become kingdoms:
dashboards, signups, endless clicking.

quest chose a smaller path.

it began as a personal way to track tasks from the shell.
then came jamz, because finding live music should feel like discovery, not admin.

---

## what it is built on

go · urfave/cli · go-pretty · jambase api · hugo + hextra
